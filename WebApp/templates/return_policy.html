Return Policy for Interior Design Products

1. Eligibility for Returns:

We want you to love your purchase! If you're not fully satisfied with your items, you can return most products within 30 days of delivery for a refund or store credit, subject to the conditions below. Some items, however, are non-returnable due to hygiene or custom-made nature.

2. Non-Returnable Items:

  • Custom or made-to-order products (e.g., personalized furniture, custom-sized rugs)
  • Clearance or final sale items
  • Gift cards or vouchers
  • Special order items or bespoke design pieces
  • Items that have been used, altered, or assembled

3. Condition of Return:

To be eligible for a return, the product must be in its original, unused condition. It must also be in the original packaging, with all tags, labels, and accessories intact.

4. Return Process:

To initiate a return, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact our customer service team at [email/phone number] with your order number and reason for return.
  2. Once your return is approved, we will provide you with a return authorization and a prepaid return shipping label.
  3. Pack the items securely in their original packaging, including any documentation and accessories.
  4. Drop off the return package at the nearest carrier location.

5. Refunds:

Refunds will be processed to the original payment method once we receive the returned item. Please allow up to 7â€“10 business days for the refund to appear in your account. The shipping costs for the return are the responsibility of the customer unless the product was defective or damaged.

6. Damaged or Defective Items:

If you receive a damaged or defective product, please contact our customer service team within 5 business days of receiving your order with photos of the damage. We will either issue a full refund or send you a replacement, including return shipping for the damaged product.

7. Exchanges:

If you wish to exchange an item, you will need to return the original item and place a new order. If the exchange is due to damage or defect, we will cover the shipping costs.

8. International Returns:

For international orders, the customer is responsible for return shipping costs. Please note that customs duties, taxes, and fees are non-refundable.
